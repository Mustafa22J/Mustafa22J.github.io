NexaReel Studio Privacy Policy

NexaReel Studio may use platform APIs to help create, review, manage, and publish short-form video content requested by the user.

We only use connected account permissions for the purpose of creating, managing, reviewing, or publishing content requested by the user.

We do not sell personal data.

API tokens and credentials are stored privately by the project owner and should not be shared publicly.

Users may revoke platform access at any time through the connected platform account settings.

NexaReel Studio may process content prompts, generated scripts, video files, captions, metadata, and publishing status information only for automation and publishing purposes.
