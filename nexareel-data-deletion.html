NexaReel Studio Data Deletion Instructions

NexaReel Studio uses connected platform permissions only to help create, review, manage, and publish short-form video content requested by the user.

If you want your data or connected account authorization removed from NexaReel Studio, please email us at nexareelstudio@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

Please include the platform account you want removed, such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest, or Snapchat.

After receiving the request, NexaReel Studio will remove locally stored access tokens, publishing credentials, and related automation data associated with that connected account where applicable.

You may also revoke app access directly from the connected platform account settings at any time.

Contact: nexareelstudio@gmail.com
